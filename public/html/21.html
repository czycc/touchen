MG animation (flat animation)

Motion Graphic simply writes MG or Mograph.Usually translated as dynamic graphics or motion graphics, usually refers to video design, multimedia CG design, TV packaging, etc.

Dynamic graphics refer to "patterns that change form over time". In simple terms, dynamic graphics can be interpreted as dynamic graphic design, which is a kind of image art.

Dynamic graphics fusion of graphic design, animation design and film language, its manifestations are rich and diverse, with a strong inclusiveness, and can be combined with various forms of expression and artistic style.

The main applications of dynamic graphics are in the package of channel, film and TV, commercial advertisement, MV, live stage screen, interactive device, etc.

MG animation history:

In 1960, John Whitney, a famous American animator, founded a company called Motion Graphics, the first to use the term "Motion Graphics".And the use of mechanical simulation computer technology to make movies and TV sets and advertisements.One of his most famous works was in 1958, in collaboration with renowned designer Saul Bass for the opening credits of Hitchcock's "Vertigo".
